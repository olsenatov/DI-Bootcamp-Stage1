  • James Potter was born on March 27, 1960 to Fleamont and Euphemia Potter.
  • He was raised in a pampered household and grew up to be proud, arrogant, and boastful, but still a good person at heart.
  • James attended Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry and was sorted into Gryffindor House.
  • He became friends with Sirius Black, Remus Lupin, and Peter Pettigrew, forming a group called the Marauders.
  • James excelled in Quidditch and became the Chaser for the Gryffindor team.
  • The Marauders discovered Remus Lupin's secret as a werewolf and learned to become Animagi to accompany him during his transformations.
  • James perfected the transformation and became a stag Animagus, earning the nickname "Prongs."
  • The Marauders created the Marauder's Map, which allowed them to navigate the Hogwarts grounds and avoid teachers.
  • James had a rivalry with Severus Snape, which started from their first meeting on the Hogwarts Express.
  • He pursued Lily Evans, but she initially found him arrogant and obnoxious, changing her mind later on.
  • James and Lily got married after graduating from Hogwarts and joined the Order of the Phoenix to fight against Voldemort.
  • They defied Voldemort's attempts to recruit them three times.
  • James inherited the Potter family fortune and used it to support his family and Remus.
  • James and Lily went into hiding when Lily became pregnant with their son, Harry.
  • They used the Fidelius Charm and made Peter Pettigrew their Secret-Keeper, but he betrayed them to Voldemort.
  • James sacrificed himself to give Lily and Harry time to escape when Voldemort attacked their home on October 31, 1981.
  • Voldemort murdered James, but Lily's sacrifice protected Harry and rebounded Voldemort's curse, resulting in his defeat and Harry becoming "the Boy Who Lived."
